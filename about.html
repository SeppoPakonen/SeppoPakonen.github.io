/ about
Seppo Pakonen is a professional music producer, composer and lyricist based in Oulu, Finland. With more than 20 years of experience with Cubase, 16 years with electric guitar, and 18 years of experience in producing music, Seppo is an excellent composer and interpreter of emotions produced by wordless music. He is a passionate professional providing services such as songwriting, producing, mixing and mastering, remote recordings and more. Get in touch to experience his skills, or check out his work to hear his musical excellence!
